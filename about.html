let me tell you mine..

My name is Vanessa. I’m 27 years old, a self-taught photographer, avid hiker, wayfarer and proud dog-mom. I call Portland, Oregon my home, but I’m on the road quite often. During the pandemic, me and my partner, finally got to build our own van, and dive into the adventures of van life. It’s been an incredible journey ever since.

We’re on the road for 5-6 months every year. Which means, even if you live on the other side of the US or beyond, we can still meet and organize a photoshoot.

more about me

I’m quite down to earth and sarcastic. I love random chats about life, traveling and creating art. When we meet, I can promise you 2 things - laughs till your stomach hurts as well as beautiful photo memories that we’ll create together.
